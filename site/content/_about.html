Ebullient is one of my favorite words, and if you know me at all, you'll know it's apropos.

I've frequently used ‘ebullient' as my IRC nick, and as women are (unfortunately still) not very common in open source IRC chat rooms, I was amused when someone referenced me as "eh-bull-i-ain't". Also, apropos.

From the dictionary (m-w.com, dictionary.com) :

e·bul·lient
adj.

  1. zestfully enthusiastic.
  2. boiling or seeming to boil; bubbling.
  3. joyously unrestrained [syn: exuberant, high-spirited]
  1. BOILING, AGITATED
  2. characterized by ebullience
  • ebul·lient·ly adverb
